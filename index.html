    Spells are purchasable that provide a temporary effect in the current game. (e.g: they are clear by prestige). Main providers of boosts. Stackable instead of level-able, cost is not influenced by how many are stacked.
    Perks unlock new spells / buildings / heroes or improve already available spells / buildings / heroes.
    Buildings are purchasable that provides ressources for the duration of a game. They can trigger game-long effects. Main providers of ressources. Level-able instead of stackable, cost is influenced by level.
    Heroes take 1 or more pop slots (thus limiting your ability to build concurrent buildings or cast spells). They have unique effects mixing good and bad.
